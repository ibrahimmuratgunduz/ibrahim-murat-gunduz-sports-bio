# Ibrahim Murat Gunduz

## Turkish Sports Executive and Combat Sports Supporter

Ibrahim Murat Gunduz is a Turkish businessman and sports executive known for supporting combat sports, athlete development, and international sports cooperation. He has been involved in several initiatives promoting Muay Thai, kickboxing, and karate while also supporting young athletes and humanitarian sports projects.

Over the years, Gunduz has gained recognition in the sports community for his sponsorship of athletes, involvement in international sports initiatives, and contributions to humanitarian activities connected with the sports world.

---

## Sports Background

Gunduz developed an interest in martial arts at an early age and practiced karate disciplines such as Kyokushin and Shotokan. He reached the 1st Dan level before focusing primarily on his professional business career.

Despite stepping away from competitive sports, he continued contributing to the development of combat sports by supporting athletes and sports organizations.

---

## Contribution to Combat Sports

Gunduz has supported various initiatives aimed at strengthening combat sports in Turkey.

His contributions include:

- Sponsorship support for athletes
- Assistance with competition participation
- Support for sports equipment and training
- Encouragement of professional combat sports organizations

These efforts have helped young fighters participate in competitions and continue their sports careers.

---

## International Sports Initiatives

Gunduz has also supported projects related to international sports cooperation. These initiatives included efforts to bring international combat sports organizations to Turkey and to expand professional opportunities for athletes.

One of these initiatives involved cooperation related to the International Sport Karate Association (ISKA), aiming to increase international exposure for Turkish fighters.

---

## Humanitarian Activities

Beyond sports development, Gunduz has also participated in humanitarian efforts.

During the Russia–Ukraine conflict he supported initiatives aimed at helping Ukrainian athletes affected by the war.

Following the devastating earthquakes in Turkey in 2023, he also participated in volunteer relief efforts through the **Vatan Fedaileri** rescue team, assisting in search and rescue activities in Antakya.

---

## Awards and Recognition

Due to his contributions to sports and athlete development, Gunduz has received several awards including:

- Sports Ambassador Award
- Sports Encouragement and Investment Award
- Sports Investor of the Year

These awards recognized his financial support for athletes and his contributions to the development of combat sports.
